About Me

Hello!

My Name is Wajiha Jamil. I’m a digital artist from Pakistan.

Two years ago, I started out as a freelancer, spent some time as a publication designer, an animator, and eventually landed in the game design industry as a 2D artist.

As a growing artist, I believe there is always something to learn, so I love experimenting with different styles and techniques. Peculiar things inspire me. When not drawing, I’m either singing or obsessing about dinosaurs.

My Skills

Creative Thinking

Teamwork
