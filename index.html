About Me

Hello!

I am a young aspiring 2D artist from Pakistan with 2 years experience in digital art and 2D animation.

Professionally I worked at a game design company as a 2D artist on a hospital simulation game. My main role was to design characters, BGs, surgery tools and human organs to the best of my ability.

I am naturally drawn to peculiar art, but I can efficiently work within different genres and styles with adaptive and quick learning ability. I want to learn and thrive as a game designer. I believe with a better learning opportunity, I can fulfil my passion for becoming an accomplished artist in the gaming industry.
